What is that brand?

The Woman Code is a recipe for a successful relationship and fun. These two elements undoubtedly build the couple's sense of happiness, which can be achieved through mutual understanding and support, which is facilitated by the Woman Code. The idea for an application that will track the menstrual cycle was born in the mind of a guy, namely Artur KsiÄ…Å¼ek - the creator of Prometheus drones.

Mission

The mission of the Woman Code is to make men aware of the great influence of hormones on the well-being of a partner during the menstrual cycle. The application not only leaves you with this awareness but also provides solutions - e.g. how to support your partner at a given time? Something like that has never happened before, Woman Code is a must-have for a modern man.

What was the problem?

We liked the creator's vision of the application from the very beginning because building a project from scratch is what we like the most. The subject of the menstrual cycle was not fully known to us, so the more we wanted to be part of the Woman Code. We took on a difficult task and the obtained effect satisfied us and our client.

What solutions have we implemented?

We've created everything in Woman Code. Read the following and you will see for yourself: - study of target groups - visual identification, logo - design and UX www - creation and publication of www - design and UX of the application - creating and publishing a total of 4 applications, one for a woman and a man on two systems - Android and iOS - advertising materials (leaflets, promotional video, advertising on social media) - from the first meeting with the client, where he told us his visio, and the actual implementation of the application was not even a year! It should be added that we did it with a total of 7 people.
