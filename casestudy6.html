IMPEL BUSINESS SOLUTIONS

What is that brand?

Impel Business Solutions are famous for its professional handling of business processes and accounting services. We have lent a hand to their success by creating an application for accounting for fleet-related expenses (e.g. leasing installments, fuel).

What was the solution used?

In the application, you can generate a CSV file, which is created based on files containing data about vehicles, and thus related expenses. What's next? The prepared CSV file is a batch file to the SAP system.

What was the end result?

The application significantly improved the efficiency of accountants who only had to verify and download the batch file to the system. Due to this solution, accounting is much faster with the least participation of the employee.
