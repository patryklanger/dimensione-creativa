What does the price of the project depend on?

It costs money to do a good IT project, we have to be clear about that. Customers often ask for the price and wonder why these services are so expensive? We will prove to you that they are not at all.

We have people on board who sit at the computer day and night, read, learn and participate in training. You are paying for this, for our experience, sleepless nights, for our passion for development. We are creative, we like original design and good quality software. When you come to us, you are guaranteed. You entrust us with your ideas and money and in return we give you quality, from the beginning to the end.

What can we give you?

QUALITY

How often do you see errors in applications or annoying solutions, unnecessary elements that distract you? Exactly, IT solutions lack quality, the final touch. Let's focus on quality first. The audience will sense if the product is good. Let's create original, non-trivial products. Let's create them together.

CREATIVITY

Our projects are top shelf, we have nothing to be ashamed of. Designers have been doing it for years and know how to delight the most sophisticated tastes. You have an idea? Share it with us and let us surprise you.

COMMITMENT

Our clients' projects are our showpiece. We will consider the project completed if we exceed your expectations.

Thanks to many years of experience, we know what steps need to be taken to create great software. This is a guarantee of the success of our projects.

Familiarize yourself with them.

1. First online conversation / live meeting - we talk with you, listen to what you have to say and ask questions about your idea.

2. Subsequent online meetings / live meetings - we create project documentation, describe all the functionalities of the future product.

3. Price and duration of the project - determining how much the project will cost and how long it will take to implement it based on the prepared documentation.

4. Creating a graphic design - competition analysis, target group analysis, content shaping, modeling, prototyping, branding, design system, adaptation to different screen resolutions.

5. Programming - creating software based on documentation and graphic design.

6. Software tests.

7. Publish.
